ABOUT ART CAPSUL

We created Art Capsul to empower everyday people to commission unique art as gifts for friends, colleagues, and family (not to mention themselves!) with minimal hassle and maximum enjoyment, and to aid up-and-coming artists in their careers, helping them gain more coverage, new clients and fans, and fair payment for their work.

Think of Art Capsul as your friendly artist and client liaison. Art Capsul delivers a safe, fun, and fair experience for all parties involved.Using Art Capsul, you can commission work from artists based on specific samples and styles of their work. This enables you to have a much better understanding of what you’ll get in return, and gives artists maximum control (though you’ll still be given a rough sketch to approve and comment on).

BROWSE THROUGH SAMPLES

Artists share samples of their work on our site that indicate the style of art you may order from them, making it very easy for you to know what you’ll be getting. We make it easy to filter and sort by price, style, and subject matter.

CHOOSE A STYLE THAT FITS YOU

When you find an artistic style you love, you’re ready to submit your order. By providing reference photos of your desired work of art’s subjects and sharing additional tips and notes, you will launch your artist on a new creative journey.

REVIEW YOUR ARTIST’S ROUGH SKETCH

To make sure you get what you want, we’ll send you a rough sketch of your made-to-order art to review and approve. This enables you to make small tweaks and correct any mistaken interpretations of your order. We’ve eliminated additional revision rounds to save time (and because your order is already based on a predetermined style)

GIVE AN UNFORGETTABLE GIFT

Few gifts are as unique and irreplaceable as original art. We make it easy to display your art as phone backgrounds, desktop wallpapers, profile images, and social media cover images, enabling you to make the most of these highly visible properties.

RECEIVE YOUR FINAL WORK OF ART

Your final work of art will be delivered directly to a private Dropbox folder (no need to wait for shipping). With a worldwide, fully-paid, royalty-free, non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-commercial, perpetual license, you are free to display your art online, order prints (independently and at lower costs), and turn your image into cards or a canvas, all the while helping your artist gain more visibility and build a more robust portfolio.

ENJOY SECURITY

If your artist turns out to be unavailable, you will get a full refund within 48 hours. You will also be refunded if your final work does not meet our standards (as judged by our artist network). You will even get a 20% refund for every late week beyond your artist’s turnaround time, as we know some orders have important gift deadlines.

HOW TO COMMISSION ART THROUGH ART CAPSUL

ART CAPSUL makes it easy to discover and order original portraits and art from up-and-coming artists with minimal hassle and risk. Our process is designed to give you exactly what you want while also letting artists maintain creative control and expand their portfolios.

ARTIST CAPSUL PURPOSE

The “ART CAPSUL” main purpose is to help the small artist to start their digital platform account or to showcase their talent online that can be seen from around the world and tobe able to sell that masterpiece they made.
